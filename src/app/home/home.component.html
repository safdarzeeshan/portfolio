Love To Eat It

your foodie adventure begins with the like button

Check it out at http://app.lovetoeat.it

Our mission is to get the world eating better by encouraging people to cook more, become a hub for recipe discovery and be at the heart of the online foodie community.

It instantly delivers recipes to a user’s inbox when they ‘like’ a food post on Instagram and adds those recipes to a user’s online personal cookbook. LTEI enables food bloggers to drive more traffic to their blogs and removes the tedious process of redirecting users through bio pages and static links in posts

Technologies used are Django Rest framework, AngularJS 1.5, Ruby, Sinatra, AWS, SASS, Grunt, PostgreSQL, Sketch, S3 bucket & Instagram API

Stereogram

Learn, Discover & Share your favorite music with your favorite friends.

Stereogram is a web app to allow friends to recommend music to each other and find out what their friends are listening to. If you're a person who likes to ask their friends for music recommendations, Stereogram gives you a platform to easily do so and never lose that song.

It leverages YouTube's and Souncloud's API for search and playback. Technologies used were Django framework, javascript, jquery and sass.
